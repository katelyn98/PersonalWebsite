Research Interests

My research interests are within the field of Computational Sustainability. If you never heard of this field before, check out CompSustNet. I am a co-chair for the CompSust DC 2020. I am interested in learning more about and researching convolutional neural nets and large scale spatio-temporal modeling/prediction along with its applications within Smart Cities, Smart Mobility, and Sustainable Development. Currently my research is centered around Smart Mobility and Smart Transporation, specifically Bike Sharing Systems. Read more about my current research project on my blog! Some other interesting topics that I have looked at in the past relate to the perception of security (specifically location services) within devleoping countries in South America.
