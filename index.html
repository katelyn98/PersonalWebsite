Impact of Signage on First Year Residence Hall Recycling

Project executed during my freshmen year at Moravian College under the supervision of Dr. Shari Dunham. After moving into the freshmen dorms, I quickly noticed the lack of recycling in my dorm. Many students did not recycle in their dorm rooms nor did they use the recycling bin in the bathroom properly. After asking around, I understood that some people did not know how to recycle and others did not even know the bin in the bathroom was for recycling. I set out to analyze this cultre on campus around recycling and to see what techniques could be done to encourage students to change.
